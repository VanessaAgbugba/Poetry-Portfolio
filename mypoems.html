Be Quiet When You Comfort Me

Do not tell me you understand
You have never known true torment
Unless you have fallen to your knees
And prayed to die
Be quiet
And do not preach the sermon of heartache
Until the day you bite your lip hard enough for it to bleed
So the walls do not hear you cry
Stop yourself from speaking of suffering my dear
You have never hurt so much
That you could not stop trembling
Still you cry out that you have known pain
Have you ever died but stayed awake
Body alive Yet soul in the grave

The Unlovables

I know a story of an abandoned few
They carry the wind in their spirit
And worship love with all their might
Yet destiny has chosen to steal from them
The only thing they have ever craved
For no matter how many prayers gush from their lips
Like shadows they must roam the world
Never going unnoticed
But perpetually ignored
And of the abandoned I am the youngest one
Still my tears brought no mercy
So let our Father's will be done
Tell our hearts not to pity us
Though we are bleeding
We are strong
I have held their hands
And with courage we accept your curse
It is a pity that the children of love
Will never be loved

On Days Like This

No one will ever know how painful it is
To write on these pages
What do you do when your heart is cut out of you
Tell me how one is supposed to heal
When pain grows fond of you
There is no one for me here
Every door is a potential gateway to hell
So i choose to spend my days with the only loves that will never leave me
I will find comfort in the paper pages
And the love of my life
Will live in my imagination
For even though my skin will never know another caress
At least my wounded heart
At last Will rest

The Scales Have Fallen

Yes the people have shown me
What I had always known
But never admitted
What I yearn for does not exist in this world
That burst of maddening addiction
The mother of all forms of affection
That rush of unexplainable emotion
As innocent as a new born’s first cry
Died with the final words
Of the crucified Christ
The last man whose heart had known real love
So darling if you seek to find such love again
Make sure your heart is unafraid of pain
For the people of your world my child
Have grown too fond of playing games

The Mythical American Dream

I remember when my greatest wish was to grace your shores, America
To stand on your soil and scream away the air from my lungs shouting
Atlast, I am here, America
With eager heart and joyous spirit I explored you
Anxiously awaiting the fulfillment of my American dream
Alas, eleven months of self education tore away the scales from my eyes
Shards of my dream America burying themselves deep in my bleeding palms
So i ask, hurt and betrayed
Why did you hide your true colors, America
Why did you not tell me the soil I stepped on was soaked in blood
You stole my brothers and sisters, America
You massaged the ears of hopeful parents with false promises

Good People Hurt The Most

Time wonders past me and I begin to dream of things beyond my reach
Paper and ink have never failed me
My emotions come to life on blank pages
Yet for so long I betrayed my source of refuge
And allowed the demons dance within me
Look closer and find a thousand cuts decorating my body
The moon peaks through my window and joins me in mourning
There is agony coursing through me
Let the world stop for a moment so I can find rest
I want to go back to the days I suckled on my mother’s breast
Because this world where only the guilty triumph
Devours the innocent
I feel my spirit failing as my body turns cold
You will forever drown in sorrow
If you keep that heart of gold

Be Quiet When You Comfort Me 2

Some moments are meant to be still and quiet
For sometimes words only cause the wound to bleed
Hold me tight in your embrace
Do not try to wipe my tear streaked face
Let the salt water flow down my face to release the pain
Let my body in your arms
Quiver and shake
Sometimes your voice is too painful to hear
But those eyes full of concern
That embrace strong and bringing life to me like air
Gives me the courage to walk the lonely road
Out of my fears

Almost

Her pulse quickened
As the blade neared its destination
And while the salty tears thick with the taste of frustration
Landed on her quivering lips
She knew a deep sleep was coming
Her shoulders have fallen to the ground
Like dust from a sandstorm
The broken pieces of her heart have pierced through her soul
So while she lays there waiting
To be sentenced to perpetual damnation
Heaven will have mercy
Because blood dripping from her wrist like liquid salvation
Was the only thing that brought her peace

The Damned

You will never be good enough
It is a wonder why you try
Have you not fallen hard too many times
There is no brightness left in that future
Before it even started you extinguished the light
Do not be stupid enough to listen to the voices in your head
Telling you to fight
You ate from the same plate as the devil
And he fed you his lies
They’ve sucked the strength away from your bones
They have left you scared and blind
But I will not cast the first stone
Because at that table I too did dine
There is no need to weep or cry
For deep within your heart
You know your only purpose
Was to be born and then die

Thou Hath Been Warned

When next you cry
Remember they told you so
But you decided to walk on the ocean
Knowing that you would drown
See this is why they mock your confidence
Because your love will never be enough
For a heart will only belong to the one it truly wants

Untitled

I feel like I belong here
Chained hand and foot to this bed
While staring back and forth at my companions
Bonding over our grief and forgotten hopes
I felt the sun on my face today
I had forgotten what it felt like to feel anything
Being locked up here with the fallen and downtrodden
Gave me more satisfaction than mere sun rays
We all hold hands here
In our minds of course
And tell our stories
Never moving our lips and yet conveying the tales of our woes
With our eyes, our tears, and occasional whimpers
Feeling our anguish in a frightening uniformity
Absurd in the eyes of outsiders
But a blissful escape to us

Untitled

It takes over you without warning
Suddenly nothing else makes you smile so widely
One single person becomes your everything
Then happiness begins to swell in your heart and fill every crack
Making you feel at peace and drunk on pure ecstacy
This is the part of love I like the most
When your chest tightens
As the butterflies in your stomach flutter faster and faster
When they are all you see
And a future without them in it seems unimaginable
Cherish this part of love
Before the ills of the world come to poison it

Untitled

Because you have my heart wrapped around your finger
Like an earthworm to a pole
That is why you take the weight on my love on your shoulders for granted
Tonight though while i wait to hear the absence of your knock on my door
I choose not to cry for myself
But to shed bitter tears for the life of someone else
I have watched you question the meaning of love
While i stood naked in front of you
The scales in your eyes were too thick to recognize
That i i was love in human form
Every section of my heart is a page that bears your name
Not a single part of my being after meeting you
Has been left unscathed, You have marked me
Even though unintentionally
I see the darkness in your eyes
While others run and hide, I dive deeper into you
For I am not too afraid to roam the dark
To seek out the light

Untitled

There’s probably another soul out there that’s just like mine
Patiently waiting in the dark to be noticed
But your limelight is too bright
Its shining in your eyes so much that it blinds you
The audience is cheering too loudly and it deafens you
I try to call out to you but my efforts are useless
Even when each scream takes all the air out of my lungs
What will happen when the encore is over
And one by one they leave the room
Will you recognise that one being who stayed behind
Or will you leave the stage
As oblivious as you were of the first soul
The same soul that walked in
Before the first note of your solo played

Tear streaked faces, quivering lips and bloodshot eyes
The dark clouds hovering above them
Pregnant with the rain of my mistakes
They cry out for me
But I am too deaf to their calls
And too blind to see their pain
Cloaked in my own hardships
I walk farther and farther
Away from the ones whom I live for
Till I fall into the welcoming arms of death's beloved angels

Untitled

We walk together in comfortable silence
Content and ignorant of the world around us
So lost are we in our own realm
That we gift former strangers our hearts
Giving them up freely, unguarded, and yearning to be loved
Now we begin to yield too easily
And cry too often
We know the source of our despair
Yet we continue to drink from its well
Drowning ourselves in a disease we are too fond of
To seek healing from
There is no hope for the likes of us
For helpless are those who fall in love

Untitled

The next time you dream of me
It will bring tears to your eyes
The medicine bottle is empty
And the ground is littered with broken glass
As beautiful as shattered ice
Tell them when you sit before my wooden chamber
That i would love my favourite songs to be played
And to be dressed in a royal blue dress
Embellished with strips of rich lace
Bring a simple honorary bouquet
And you will see my smiling face
All gathered will feel my presence
For i was the love that was always true
And all though i roam no more
A piece of me will remain forever
Dwelling in all of you

Only to watch the cold wind of loneliness erase it from existence
A heavy heart will do you no good
It is the way of the world
To surround you with many

Untitled

Fire and ice were never meant to mix
But i would cry along with you
At the sight of your red eyes
Your evil would not let my love fade
My wounds could be reopened a thousand times
And i would bleed blood and water
But still stay
If i could bathe in a cauldron of fire to ease your pain
I would wallow there for an eternity
If only to stop a tear from flowing down your face
I do not love like the rest of the world
I’d cherish you more than i cherished myself
The thought of your discomfort would feel worse than hell
Still the world’s love is shallow and dead
So tell me where will i find a love that is heaven sent
On earth
